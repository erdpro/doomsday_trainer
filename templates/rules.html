Explanation of rules to calculate any day.

Doomsdays:

  • January (3/1 or 4/1 if leap year)
  • February (28/2 or 29/2 if leap year)
  • March (14/3 and 7/3)
  • April (4/4)
  • May (9/5)
  • June (6/6)
  • July (11/7)
  • August (8/8)
  • September (5/9)
  • October (10/10)
  • November (7/11)
  • December (12/12) and (26/12)

Year starts:

  • 1800: 5 - Friday
  • 1900: 3 - Wednesday
  • 2000: 2 - Tuesday
  • 2100: 0 - Sunday

Years:

  • Every 4 years add one extra for a leap year
  • Every 12 years = 1 (0, 12, 24, 36, 48, 60, 72, 84, 96)
  • Every 28 years = 0 (0, 28, 56, 84)
  • Only 2000 is a leap year (happens every 400 years at the start of a century)

Days of the week:

  • 0 - Sunday (Noneday)
  • 1 - Monday (Oneday)
  • 2 - Tuesday (Twosday)
  • 3 - Wednesday (Threesday)
  • 4 - Thursday (Foursday)
  • 5 - Friday (Fivesday)
  • 6 - Saturday (Sixaday)
